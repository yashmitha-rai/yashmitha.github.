About Us

Founded in 2010, Rose Haven Girls' Hostel was born out of the need for safe and reliable accommodation for women in the city. Managed by a dedicated team of female staff, security personnel, and caretakers, our hostel upholds the highest standards of hygiene, safety, and hospitality.

Our Vision

To create a supportive space where women can thrive academically, professionally, and personally.

Why Choose Us?

  • 24/7 CCTV & security guards
  • Nutritious meals prepared by in-house chefs
  • Modern, well-furnished rooms
  • Inclusive and respectful community
